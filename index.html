All| Proprietary| Partner Solutions| Open Source Software

STarget

STarget is a sentiment analysis technology for detection of polarity of texts written in a natural language. It works at multiple levels of granularity, supports many subject domains and languages.

Opinosis

A novel graph-based summarization framework that generates concise abstractive summaries of highly redundant texts. Perfect for summarizing reviews on an e-commerce website or comments for a news article or a popular blog.

[job]Snipper

Technology to fully automatically synthesize highly informative structured snippets from unstructured job postings. Allows to improve UX on a search engine result page (SERP) and mobile devices.

CamRecSys

A breakthrough technology from Cambridge that allows to seemlesly combine multiple unstructured heterogeneous data sources and work with them in a unified way to build search and recommednations solutions. It demonstrates state-of-the-art performance on public benchmarks.

RichRelevance

Having an exclusive partnership with RichRelevance for CIS market, we offer SaaS omnichannel personalization services, numerous recommendation solutions, and analytical infrastructure from a global leader to the most innovative e-commerce companies in the region.

Gravity R&D

Having an exclusive partnership with Gravity R&D for CIS market, we bring guaranteed extra sales and increased user satisfaction to the companies in the region. Uncover hidden relationships between personal tastes of your users through sophisticated mathematical algorithms.

Parboiled2

Parboiled2 is a Scala 2.10.3+ library enabling lightweight and easy-to-use, yet powerful, fast and elegant parsing of arbitrary input text. It implements a macro-based parser generator for PEGs, which runs at compile time and translates a grammar rule definition into JVM bytecode.
